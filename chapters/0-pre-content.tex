\begin{abstract}
  \begin{enumerate}
      \item Talk about general background of exoskeleton as an assistive device for people with impaired or lost motor functions or for rehabilitation. Put emphasize on the control and the need for accurate real-time control. 
      \item Talk about myoelectric control, EMG-based models to predict joint torque, and how to make it real-time. State my research hypothesis which is that ANN algorithm could be used for EMG pattern recognition to allow real-time myoelectric control of joint torques.
      \item Talk about methodology, i.e. motion capture > Vicon postprocessing > OpenSim and CEINMS postprocessing inverse dynamics to retrieve the joint torques which are the training labels for the network > type of neural network
      \item Talk about the results and implications
  \end{enumerate}
